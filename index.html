Diwali

Diwali is a festival of lights and one of the major festivals celebrated by Hindus, Jains and Sikhs. The festival lasts five days and celebrated during the Hindu month Kartika

More On Diwali

Holi

Holi is a popular ancient Hindu festival, also known as the Indian "festival of spring", the "festival of colours", and the "festival of love". The festival signifies the triumph of good over evil

More On Holi

Vaisakhi

Vaisakhi, also pronounced as Baisakhi is a historical and religious festival in Sikhism. It is usually celebrated on 13 or 14 April every year, and celebrates the start of the month of Vaisakha

More On Vaisakhi

Pongal

Pongal is a multi-day Harvest festival observed by the Tamil community. It is observed at the start of the month Tai according to Tamil solar calendar, and on typically about January 14

More On Pongal

Lohri

Lohri is a Punjabi festival celebrated by Dogra and Punjabi communities. It is believed by many that the festival commemorates the passing of the winter solstice

More On Lohri
